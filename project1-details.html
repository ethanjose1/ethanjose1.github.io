Project Overview

During my IST387 data science class, we were tasked with being a consultant for a made-up energy company based in North Carolina and South Carolina. Our goal was to explore factors related to energy usage and make scientific predictions based on a 5-degree increase in temperature to prepare for climate change.

Business Questions Addressed:

  1. What factors relate to the consumption of energy in North Carolina and South Carolina?
  2. What factors relate to the cooling of different types of homes during the hot months of the summer?
  3. What climate factors strongly correlate to energy efficiency?
  4. What physical factors relating to a house correlate to energy efficiency? (e.g., square footage, layout, windows)
  5. What types of insulation and building materials correlate to higher energy usage?
  6. What are the most important factors to be concerned about given a five-degree increase in average temperature?
